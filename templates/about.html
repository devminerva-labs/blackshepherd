Our Mission

4 Quality Education
5 Gender Equality
8 Decent Work

Aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals, our mission is to achieve gender equality (SDG 5) by ensuring inclusive and equitable quality education for every girl (SDG 4), and promoting sustained, inclusive economic growth with full and productive employment for all women (SDG 8).

We execute this by funding education, building skills for decent work, creating business opportunities, and guaranteeing access to critical support services for health and safety, empowering women and girls to claim a future of autonomy and power.
