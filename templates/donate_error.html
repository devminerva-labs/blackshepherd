We encountered an issue processing your donation

What happened?

Your payment could not be processed at this time. This could be due to various reasons, and we're here to help you complete your donation.

Don't worry - no money has been charged to your account.

Common causes include:

  • Insufficient funds or daily spending limit reached
  • Card information entered incorrectly
  • Bank security measures blocking the transaction
  • Temporary network or system issues
  • Card expired or blocked
  • International transaction restrictions

Try These Steps

  1. 1
    Check your card details
    Verify card number, expiry date, CVV, and billing address are correct
  2. 2
    Try a different card
    Use another debit/credit card or payment method
  3. 3
    Contact your bank
    Your bank may have blocked the transaction for security reasons
  4. 4
    Try again later
    Wait a few minutes and retry your donation
